== Description ==

Holiday Message allows you to add a popup message along with a holiday graphic, over the top or at the bottom of your site.

Choose between a number of holiday graphics, add a message and/or a countdown. Then change the colours, position and even add a snowfall effect, perfect for letting your visitors know about your Christmas offer or your office opening times over the festive season.

Happy Holidays.

* [Graphics by Dean Barrett](https://deanbarrett.wordpress.com)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `holiday-message` folder to your to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to Settings -> Holiday Message and make your changes
4. Click the "Preview Message on site" link to preview your message
4. Once happy with the preview, click the `Save Changes` button
